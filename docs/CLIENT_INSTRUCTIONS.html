1. Overview

What is NEXA?

NEXA Platform is an online shopping system where each store has its own website. The main site helps people find stores; each vendor has a separate shop. Customers shop one store at a time.

User roles

RoleResponsibility
CustomerBrowse, cart, checkout, track orders
VendorManage one store — products, orders, settings
Super AdminManage the whole platform

Where customers shop

Customers do not shop from a product list on the main homepage. Open a vendor store link (e.g. https://gogo.nexa-mm.com/) or click a vendor logo on the homepage carousel.

2. Customer guide

Opening a store

MethodExample
Vendor subdomainhttps://gogo.nexa-mm.com/
Custom domainhttps://yourstore.com/
Main site carouselClick a vendor logo

Browsing and cart

  • Use category tabs and search to find products.
  • Tap a product for details; use Add to Cart or the heart icon for saved items.
  • Guest carts stay on one device; sign in to sync across devices.

Checkout

  1. Open Cart → Checkout.
  2. Enter shipping address and phone.
  3. Choose Cash on Delivery or KBZPay.
  4. Review and place the order.

Cash on Delivery

Order is created immediately. Pay the delivery person in cash when your order arrives. Track status under Profile → Orders when signed in.

KBZPay

  1. Choose KBZPay at checkout.
  2. Pay in the KBZPay app (QR or app launch).
  3. After payment you may see https://www.nexa-mm.com/summary — tap Continue Shopping.

Keep the browser open until payment finishes. If payment succeeded but no order appears, contact the store with your receipt.

Profile, support, and shortcuts

  • Profile: orders, addresses, account settings.
  • Support: chat bubble; phone menu for Dial or Viber.
  • Add to Home: Android Chrome — tap the button above chat; iPhone — Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen.
  • Language: English or Burmese on the storefront menu.

3. Vendor guide

Getting started

  1. Apply at https://www.nexa-mm.com/vendor/application.
  2. Wait for approval from super admin.
  3. Sign in at https://www.nexa-mm.com/vendor/login and open vendor admin.
URLPurpose
https://yourstore.nexa-mm.com/Customer shop
https://yourstore.nexa-mm.com/adminYour dashboard

Application requirements

FieldRequirement
PhoneMyanmar format: +959… or 09…
EmailWait for Email is available
DescriptionAt least 10 characters

Daily work

  • Products: add photos, prices, stock, and categories.
  • Orders: check pending orders; update status as you fulfill.
  • Settings: logo, banner, contact, terms, subdomain.
  • Use Preview store before sharing your link.

4. Super admin guide

Access

Open https://www.nexa-mm.com/admin and sign in with your staff account.

Daily workflow

  1. Review Orders (pending badge on sidebar).
  2. Recover KBZPay drafts from the amber panel if needed.
  3. Approve or reject vendors under Vendor → Review applications.
  4. Check Settings → Activities for the audit log.
