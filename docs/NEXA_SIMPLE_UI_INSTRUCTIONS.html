NEXA Platform — Simple UI instructions

For: anyone using the admin or vendor screens — step by step, plain words.

2) Press Ctrl+P (Windows) or Cmd+P (Mac).

Words you will see

WordWhat it means
Super Admin / AdminThe main control room for the whole shopping site.
VendorA seller who has their own mini-shop.
StorefrontThe public website where customers browse and buy.
Vendor adminThe private dashboard for one vendor.

Part A — Super Admin (whole platform)

A1. Open admin

  1. Open your web browser.
  2. Go to your NEXA Platform site and add /admin at the end of the address.
    Example: https://yoursite.com/admin
  3. Sign in with the email and password your owner gave you.

A2. What is on the screen

  • Left: the main menu (list of sections).
  • Top: search, notifications, your name / profile.
  • Middle: the work area — it changes when you click the menu.

A3. Menu — what to click

MenuWhat it is for
HomeSummary / dashboard.
ProductSubmenu: Product, Categories, Inventory — what is sold and stock.
OrdersAll orders.
VendorSeller partners; Review applications for new sellers.
Promo SettingDiscounts / promotions.
ChatCustomer messages.
CustomersCustomer list.
FinancesMoney / reports (if your login allows).
LogisticsDelivery partners (shipping companies).
SettingsGeneral (name, logo), Users (staff), Activities (audit log).

If you do not see Finances or Settings, your account is limited on purpose.

A4. Vendors (short steps)

  1. Click Vendor on the left.
  2. Use the search box to find a vendor.
  3. Use the filter if you need only certain statuses.
  4. Use Review applications for new signup requests — there is no manual Add vendor button.
  5. On a row, the ⋮ (three dots) menu may open profile or storefront preview.

A5. Settings & team (owners)

  1. Click Settings.
  2. Use tabs for General (name, logo), Users (staff), and Activities (platform audit timeline).
  3. To add staff: Users → Add user → fill form → save.

Part B — Vendors (sellers)

B1. Login

  1. Open: yoursite.com/vendor/login (use your real site name).
  2. Enter email and password for the vendor account.

B2. Vendor admin link (bookmark this)

Examples:

  • yoursite.com/vendor/YOUR-STORE-SLUG/admin
  • yoursite.com/store/YOUR-STORE-SLUG/admin

YOUR-STORE-SLUG is your shop short name. Ask Super Admin if unsure.

B3. Vendor menu

MenuUse it to…
Analytics / DashboardSee overview.
Products → All productsAdd or edit products.
Products → CategoriesOrganize categories.
OrdersSee your store’s orders.
CustomersSee buyers (as the system shows).
FinancesEarnings / money views.
SettingsStore name, logo, link, storefront look.

B4. Preview the public shop

  1. In vendor admin, find Preview / View storefront (or similar).
  2. Click — a new tab shows what customers see.

B5. Link for customers

Share the link your team confirms, often like:

  • yoursite.com/vendor/YOUR-SLUG or
  • yoursite.com/store/YOUR-SLUG
